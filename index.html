// About Us

Powering Digital Futures

A Message from the Co-Founder

Good day, everyone. I'm Engr. David Solomon, co-founder of SolexGift Tech Academy alongside my partner Gift — both proud indigenes of Okokolo Agatu, Benue State.

We created SolexGift Tech Academy with a single mission: to democratize technology education right here in our community. Every young person in Benue deserves access to world-class digital skills that can unlock opportunities both locally and globally.

This platform gives you access to video courses, live lectures, practical workshops, and industry-recognized certifications in Web Development, Data Analysis, Graphic Design, Video Editing, Office Packages, and more.

Learning at SolexGift is not just theory — it is hands-on, outcome-focused, and community-driven. We are honored to have you here, and we promise your journey will be transformative.
