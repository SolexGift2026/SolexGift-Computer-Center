About Us

Good day everyone.

My name is Engr. David Solomon, a co-founder of SolexGift Tech Academy, alongside my beautiful lady, Gift. We are both proud indigenes of Okokolo Agatu community in Benue State.

It is with great joy that we welcome you to SolexGift Tech Academy. Here, we focus on practical teaching and real skills acquisition in technology. Our mission is to empower young minds and individuals with hands-on knowledge that can create opportunities and build a better future.

At SolexGift Tech Academy, this platform gives you access to a lot of educational videos that will help boost your tech knowledge in areas such as website design and development, data analysis, video editing, office packages, and many more.

At SolexGift Tech Academy, learning is not just theory — we believe in practice, innovation, and excellence. We are glad to have you with us, and we assure you that your journey here will be impactful and rewarding.
